Our History

Founded in 2012, Durban Animal Rescue (DAR) began as a small group of volunteers focused on rescuing strays in local communities. Today we coordinate adoptions, sterilisation campaigns, and education programmes.

Mission & Vision

  • Mission: Protect and rehome vulnerable animals.
  • Vision: A community where all animals are cared for and no pet is left homeless.

Our Team

Trained volunteers, foster families, and partner veterinarians form the heart of DAR.
